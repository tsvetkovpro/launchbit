Sure, I agree… I was rather sloppy in writing this post (so many typos that I don’t have time to fix). Yes, creative does matter for all cases. You can’t just put out a bad ad.

What I meant to say is that, you care less about trying to maximize clicks under PPC platforms than under flat rate or CPM. If you’re paying CPM, you really want your creative to stand out.

Yes, you pay more on a CPC system if your CTR is lower. But again, another sloppy mistake on my part — I should’ve said lower w/ respect to your competitor ads. A good portion of CTR is actually attributed to ad format.

If you’re paying on CPC, say on Google AdWords, you care less about standing out. Your ad just needs to do better than other ads that could be going in that slot, but it doesn’t mean you have to go above and beyond crazy to stand out.
