We're On it.

During the late 90s, my best friend (and now co-founder) Jennifer and I programmed for a variety of startups in the Silicon Valley, where we grew up. It was an exciting time to be high schoolers working on innovative new products and ideas. But, all too often, we saw a lot of startups fail, because they did not have enough customers.

Years later, in 2011, we created LaunchBit to be a place where we could build great technology products with amazing people. We want to help B2B companies become successful and continue to become even more successful by helping them get more customers in simple ways using our software platform. Our software helps B2B marketers generate new leads from their target audiences.

Today, we work with fantastic companies ranging from small startups to Fortune 500 companies. We're backed by an amazing set of investors from the Silicon Valley and are proud to work with a great team. We'd love to get to know you, too. Drop us a line at hello [at] launchbit [dot] com.

Elizabeth Yin

LaunchBit, CEO & Co-Founder
